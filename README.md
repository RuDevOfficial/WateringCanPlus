## Watering Cans
![](https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_f2a35e2a-3c8d-4db8-932c-bda8cc25d0d7.png)

*   **Copper**: Has only <span style="color: #e03e2d;">10</span> durability, waters only <span style="color: #e03e2d;">1</span> block at a time. <span style="color: #b96ad9;">Unlocked at the start.</span>

<span style="color: #b96ad9;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_be760266-5ad0-4095-8936-78f91ea0e0fd.png"></span>

*   **Gold**: Has <span style="color: #2dc26b;">35</span> durability but waters only <span style="color: #e03e2d;">1</span> block at a time. <span style="color: #b96ad9;"> Unlocked at tier 2.</span>

<span style="color: #b96ad9;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_82a181bf-cfcd-42aa-a0b6-0f113e27258d.png"></span>

*   **Thorium**: Has 20 durability but waters in <span style="color: #2dc26b;">2x2</span> blocks at a time. <span style="color: #b96ad9;">Unlocked at tier 4.</span>

<span style="color: #b96ad9;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_bac27a0b-7e11-45c6-9010-a91169d56c4a.png"></span>

*   **Cobalt**: Has 20 durability but waters in <span style="color: #2dc26b;"> 2x2</span> blocks at a time. <span style="color: #b96ad9;"> Unlocked at tier 4.</span>

<span style="color: #b96ad9;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_1c0a1bff-9975-48ed-bf65-190ee1f04e37.png"></span>

*   **Adamantite**: Has 20 durability but waters in <span style="color: #2dc26b;">3x3 </span> blocks at a time. <span style="color: #b96ad9;"> Unlocked at tier 5.</span>

<span style="color: #b96ad9;"><img src="https://media.forgecdn.net/attachments/description/null/description_80668451-c0c0-44d5-b250-35c82db618da.png"></span>

*   **Mithril**: Has 20 durability but waters in <span style="color: #2dc26b;">4x4</span> blocks at a time. <span style="color: #b96ad9;"> Unlocked at tier 6.</span>
